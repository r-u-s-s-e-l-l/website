A Block

When I first thought of starting up my own blog, there was an extensive list of words I wanted to write on.

But as I finally start to work on an idea that’s been rotting away at the back of my head, I find it to be a room void of life. It's contents escaped from a cage of suffocating pretentious. Perhaps I have consciously chosen to avoid the fear of inconclusive speculation, the profound trains of thought that are misleadingly destination-bound.

However, that might not be such a bad thing.
